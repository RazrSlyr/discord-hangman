Hangman UNSPOILERED!

RULES

Hangman UNSPOILERED! is a version of Hangman that you can play using discord messages! The size and shape of the word is signified by the number of "?" on top. To guess a letter, unspoiler all entries on that letter's row. If a letter is present in the word, then at least one of spot on its row will contain the letter. The remaining will be green squares. If a letter is not present in the word, all entries in the row will be red squares. If you guess a letter and it is not present, unspoiler one element of the "hangman" below. If you unspoiler all the letters in the word before you unspoiler all of the hangman, then you win! If you unspoiler all of the hangman before you unspoiler all the letters in the word, then you lose. :(

PLAY!

To play, simply enter your word or phrase in the textbox below, and click generate! Then, copy and paste the two Discord messages!
